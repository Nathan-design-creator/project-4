UNIVERSITY OF NIGERIA SECONDARY SCHOOL

No. 244 Umukanka Street, Nsukka, Enugu State, Nigeria.

It has about over 7000 students enrolled in its citadel of learning.


In its junior classes, students offer all subjects which are 17 in number while in senior classes, students differ (science /art students) who offer either 13 or 14 subjects each.
It is registered under the West African Examination Council (WAEC).
